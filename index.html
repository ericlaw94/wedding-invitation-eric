疑问和解答

Q & A

Can I bring a date?

Please check your invite for your +1!

请查看您的邀请函以确认是否附有同伴名额

Where should I park?

The premises will provide car parking. Parking is complimentary.

场地提供停车位，停车免费。

What should I wear?

There is no dress code, but we recommend wearing pastel colors for the best photo moments!

没有着装要求，但建议穿着浅色系服装，拍照效果更佳

Is the wedding indoors or outdoors?

Both! All areas are air-conditioned. Outdoor areas are sheltered, so you won't be affected by rain.

室内及室外所有区域均设有空调。室外区域设有遮蔽，不受雨天影响。

How will food and drinks be served?

Food and drinks will be served buffet-style.

餐饮将以自助餐形式供应。

What if I have a dietary restriction?

Our buffet is fully halal and offers a wide variety of options, including vegetarian, seafood, and sashimi. However, we are unable to accommodate gluten-free dietary needs. If you have any concerns, please let us know when you RSVP.

我们的自助餐全程供应清真食品，提供多种选择，包括素食、海鲜及刺身。但目前无法提供无麸质餐食选项。如有任何疑问，请在回复出席确认时告知我们。

What time should I arrive?

Please refer to the schedule provided in the invitation and arrive accordingly.

请参照邀请函中的时间安排准时到达。

Will I have to pay for parking?

No, complimentary parking is provided for all guests.

不需要，所有宾客均可享有免费停车

Does your wedding have a theme?

Yes, our wedding is a garden-themed celebration.

有的，我们的婚礼以花园主题为概念。

What time will the reception end?

The reception is expected to end at 9:30 PM.

婚宴预计于晚上9点30分结束。

Are there any colors that guests should avoid wearing?

There are no color restrictions, but we recommend wearing pastel colors — they'll look great in photos with our venue's lighting!

没有颜色限制，但建议穿着柔和的浅色彩色系服装——搭配我们场地的灯光效果，拍照会更加出彩！

Is there any alcohol provided?

Alcohol will not be provided, but you're welcome to bring your own!

现场不提供酒精饮品，但欢迎自行携带！
